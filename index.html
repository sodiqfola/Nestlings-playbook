About

The Nestlings Playbook is a pioneer project by the Nestlings ( A strategic partnership between CIC and The NEST) to actively contribute to Africa’s transition to a digital economy by increasing the inclusion and participation of the undergraduate community in the innovation ecosystem of the African economy. It is a community-driven initiative that aims to transform university undergraduates in Nigeria into thriving innovators and entrepreneurs. Our vision is to incubate, empower and equip youths with the right tools and resources needed to build, launch, grow, and scale their ideas and businesses while still in school.

Through our hands-on training, mentorship, and access to funding, we are enabling the best generation of African changemakers to create innovative solutions that address the continent's most pressing challenges as a contribution to unlocking its prosperous destiny. We are calling for Applications

Apply to participate in the The Nestlings Playbook challenge to access up to $15,000 in investment and other benefits like co-working space, advisory services, Mentors, development support, founder community and other resources.

Your application will provide you the opportunity to participate in the upcoming Nestlings Venture Case Challenge to get into the Nestlings Incubator.
